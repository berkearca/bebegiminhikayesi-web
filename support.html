Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

On your iPhone, open the Settings app, tap your name at the top, then go to Subscriptions and select My Baby Story to cancel. If you cancel during the free trial, you won't be charged.

Where is my data stored?

All your memories and photos are kept only on your device. They are never sent to our servers.

How do I move to a new phone?

You can create a backup file from within the App and restore it on your new device. iCloud sync is planned for a future update.

What does Premium include?

Unlimited memories, all firsts, photo collages, and the year in review. Try it free for 7 days and cancel anytime.
