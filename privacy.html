Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

My Baby Story ("the App") is offered by Tuba Üstün. We care about your privacy. This policy explains, in plain language, what information is handled and how.

Your data stays on your device

Everything you add to the App (your baby's name and birth details, notes, memories, and photos) is stored only on your device. This data is never sent to our servers, never seen by us, and never collected.

Photos

When you choose to add a photo to a memory, only the photos you select are accessed. Photos are kept inside the App's own space on your device and are never uploaded.

No account needed

The App does not require an account or sign-in. There is no login, and we never ask for or store your name or email to use the App.

Subscriptions and payments

Premium subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. We have no access to your payment or card details; these are handled entirely by Apple.

No tracking, no ads

The App contains no third-party ad networks, tracking, or analytics software. We do not monitor your behavior.

Children's privacy

Baby information in the App is entered and controlled by a parent or caregiver. All of it stays on the device, and no data is collected directly from children.

Deleting your data

When you remove the App from your device, all of its data and photos are deleted with it. You can also delete individual memories at any time from within the App.

Your rights

Because your data lives on your device, access and deletion are entirely in your control. Please contact us with any privacy questions.
